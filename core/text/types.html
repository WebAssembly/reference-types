Result Types¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\mathdef2376#1{{}}\mathdef2376{result type} & \href{../text/types.html#text-resulttype}{\mathtt{resulttype}} &::=& (t{:}\href{../text/types.html#text-functype}{\mathtt{result}})^? &\Rightarrow& [t^?] \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Note

In future versions of WebAssembly, this scheme may be extended to support multiple results or more general result types.

Abbreviations¶

Multiple anonymous parameters or results may be combined into a single declaration:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef2376#1{{}}\mathdef2376{parameter} & \def\mathdef2427#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2427{(}~~\def\mathdef2428#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2428{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef2429#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2429{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef2430#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2430{(}~~\def\mathdef2431#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2431{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef2432#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2432{)})^\ast \\ \def\mathdef2376#1{{}}\mathdef2376{result} & \def\mathdef2433#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2433{(}~~\def\mathdef2434#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2434{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef2435#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2435{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef2436#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2436{(}~~\def\mathdef2437#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2437{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef2438#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2438{)})^\ast \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
